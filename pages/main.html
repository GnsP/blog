The Blog that came too late

The primary purpose of this Blog is to promote and showcase the simplicity of design, be it User Interface and Experience (UI/UX) design or software design or artistic design in general. Here I take a lazy stance in terms of design, no fancy effects. The idea is to keep the Blog clean, minimal, pleasant and content oriented.

This Blog uses the ghournal blog engine in the back-end, if we can call it a back-end. This Blog in its entirity is a Single Page Application (SPA) written in AngularJS and hosted as a github-page.

This Blog is not entirely technical. There are poems, stories, drawings and a lot of random thoughts too. This Blog is the way I look at the world, the way I feel about everything.

Where is the footer !

Do we really need a footer ? How many visitors really care about what you write in the footer ? Therefore, I decided to design this Blog without a traditional bulky footer section. Though, we do have a single line of text with link to my homepage here.
